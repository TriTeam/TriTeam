Game Basics

What is TriTeam Fantasy?

TriTeam Fantasy is a fantasy sports game where you build your dream team of triathletes for upcoming races. Each competitor has a value, and you have a budget to build your team. Your team earns points based on the real-life performance of your selected competitors in races.

1

Select a Race

Choose from upcoming triathlon races in the calendar.

2

Build Your Team

Select 4 male and 4 female competitors within your $100 budget for each category.

3

Earn Points

Your team earns points based on the real race results.

4

Compete

Compare your score with friends and other players on the leaderboard.

Game Timeline

Race Announcement

New races are added to the calendar with competitor lists.

Team Selection Period

Build and modify your team until the lockout time.

Lockout (8:00 AM Race Day)

Team selections are locked, and no further changes can be made.

Race Day

Real-world race takes place.

Results & Points

Race results are processed, and points are awarded to teams.

Team Building

Team Structure

For each race, you must build two separate teams:

Men's Team

  • 2 Gold competitors (100% points)
  • 2 Silver competitors (50% points)
  • $100 total budget

Women's Team

  • 2 Gold competitors (100% points)
  • 2 Silver competitors (50% points)
  • $100 total budget

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Waiting until the last minute: Build your team early and make adjustments as needed before the lockout.
  • Ignoring Silver competitors: While they earn fewer points, the right Silver picks can significantly boost your total score.
  • Not using your full budget: Always aim to use your entire $100 budget to maximize potential points.
  • Picking based on name recognition only: Focus on current form and race-specific factors rather than just selecting well-known competitors.
  • Forgetting to save your team: Always click "Save Team" after making your selections to ensure your team is registered for the race.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q

What happens if a competitor I selected doesn't start or finish the race?

A

If a competitor doesn't start or doesn't finish (DNS/DNF), they will receive 0 points for that race.

Q

Can I change my team after the lockout time?

A

No, once the lockout time (8:00 AM on race day) has passed, no changes can be made to your team for that race.

Q

How do I invite friends to play?

A

Use the "Share on WhatsApp" button at the bottom of the page to invite friends to join TriTeam Fantasy.

Q

How often are competitor values updated?

A

Competitor values are typically updated after each race based on their performance and results.

Q

Do I need to create a team for every race?

A

Yes, you need to create a new team for each race. Your selections don't carry over automatically.

Q

What if there's a tie in points on the leaderboard?

A

In case of a tie, the player who has participated in more races will be ranked higher. If that's also tied, the player with the highest single-race score will be ranked higher.

Q
